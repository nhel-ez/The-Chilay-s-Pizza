    • CHEESY SISIG PIZZA

    Ingredients:

    1, GREEN BELL PEPPER, DICED
    1, ONION, SLICED INTO RINGS
    3 TBSP, TOMATO SAUCE
    1, READY MADE PIZZA DOUGH
    350G, PORK, CUBED
    200G, LIVER, CUBED
    A PINCH, SALT
    A PINCH, PEPPER
    1 TBSP, EDEN MAYO
    1/2 CUP, EDEN ORIGINAL, GRATED
    2 TBSP, CALAMANSI JUICE

    Procedures:

    1. Grill pork and liver until brown. Set aside.
    2. In a bowl, prepare sisig by combining pork and liver, bell pepper and onion rings.
    3. Pour calamansi juice and mix in Eden Mayo.
    4. Mix well.
    5. Prepare pizza by evenly spreading the tomato sauce on the pizza dough.
    6. Top with sisig and grated Eden cheese.
    7. Bake for 15 minutes at 350F.
    8. Serve and enjoy!
