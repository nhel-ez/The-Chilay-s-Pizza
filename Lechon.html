    • CHEESY LECHON PIZZA

    Ingredients:

    1 GREEN BELL PEPPER, DICED
    1, ONION, SLICED INTO RINGS
    3 TBSP, TOMATO SAUCE
    1, READY MADE PIZZA DOUGH
    TO TASTE, SALT
    TO TASTE, PEPPER
    2 CUPS, EDEN MELT SARAP, GRATED
    350G, LECHON, CHOPPED INTO STRIPS

    Procedures:

    1.In a bowl, combine lechon with bell peppers and onion.
    2. Prepare pizza by spreading tomato sauce evenly.
    3. Top with lechon mix and grated cheese.
    4. Bake at 350F for 15 minutes. Serve and enjoy!
